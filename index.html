Hi, I am Maayan.
Last August my family had a great trip time in Romania.
We truveled me, my husband Shlomi, and our cute children:
Ayala (10.5), Ori (10.5), Dvir (9) and Elad (3.5).
For the children it was the first time abroad, so it was such an exited experience for them.
Here are some of the best recommended places we visited:

Herastrau park

A big park in Bucharest full of gardens around a beautiful lake.
We enjoyed a half a day walking, sailing a rawboat and playing around the park.

Sinaia cable car

Sinaia is a town and a mountain resort in Romania, far about 2 hours driving from Bucharest.
In this beautiful pastoral place we took two cable cars, to the summit of 2050m height,
and enjoyed the way up and the wonderful view on top.

Aventura Park

A great rope and omegas park, located in Brassov town, full of adventures for both children and adults.
We spent there a whole day enjoiyng the various challenges.
To Aventura Park web site

Balea lake

Balea lake is a beautiful pearl of the nature.
The way to there is an adventure by itself, passes the spacial Transfagarasan rode.
The continuance rode known by the bears families that live there.

In summary, this is a little tasting from our trip.
It was was fun, and challenging too.
